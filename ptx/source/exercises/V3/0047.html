Consider the statement

The set of vectors \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 18 \\ 20 \\ 14 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 5 \\ 8 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 4 \\ -1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] \right\} \)does not span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).

  1. Write an equivalent statement using a vector equation.
  2. Explain why your statement is true or false.

  1. The statement

    The set of vectors \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 18 \\ 20 \\ 14 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 5 \\ 8 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 4 \\ -1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] \right\} \) does not span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).
