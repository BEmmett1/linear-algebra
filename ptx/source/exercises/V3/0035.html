Consider the statement

The set of vectors \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -3 \\ -1 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 3 \\ -5 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -4 \\ -3 \\ -5 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] \right\} \)does not span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).

  1. Write an equivalent statement using a vector equation.
  2. Explain why your statement is true or false.
