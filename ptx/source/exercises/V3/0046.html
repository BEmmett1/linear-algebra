Consider the statement

The set of vectors \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -5 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -10 \\ 10 \\ 18 \\ 28 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 30 \\ -2 \\ -26 \\ -49 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] \right\} \)does not span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).

  1. Write an equivalent statement using a vector equation.
  2. Explain why your statement is true or false.
