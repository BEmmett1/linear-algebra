Consider the statement

The set of vectors \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -5 \\ -2 \\ 4 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -5 \\ -1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 14 \\ 24 \\ -4 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -27 \\ -43 \\ 9 \\ 14 \end{array}\right] \right\} \)does not span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).

  1. Write an equivalent statement using a vector equation.
  2. Explain why your statement is true or false.
