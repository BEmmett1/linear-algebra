Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -4 & -3 & -1 & -5 \\ -1 & 5 & -4 & 0 \\ 6 & -6 & 6 & -3 \\ 0 & 1 & -2 & 0 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -4 & -3 & -1 & -5 \\ -1 & 5 & -4 & 0 \\ 6 & -6 & 6 & -3 \\ 0 & 1 & -2 & 0 \end{array}\right] = 243 \]
