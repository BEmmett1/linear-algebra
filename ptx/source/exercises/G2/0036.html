Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 3 & -2 & 2 & 0 \\ 2 & -5 & 5 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -3 & -1 \\ 2 & 3 & -4 & 2 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 3 & -2 & 2 & 0 \\ 2 & -5 & 5 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -3 & -1 \\ 2 & 3 & -4 & 2 \end{array}\right] = 33 \]
