Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & -2 & 3 & -6 \\ 4 & -6 & 6 & 4 \\ 5 & 5 & -3 & -5 \\ 3 & 0 & 1 & 0 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & -2 & 3 & -6 \\ 4 & -6 & 6 & 4 \\ 5 & 5 & -3 & -5 \\ 3 & 0 & 1 & 0 \end{array}\right] = 146 \]
