Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & -6 & -2 & 5 \\ -3 & 0 & 0 & -1 \\ 6 & -3 & -4 & 3 \\ 5 & -2 & -1 & -2 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & -6 & -2 & 5 \\ -3 & 0 & 0 & -1 \\ 6 & -3 & -4 & 3 \\ 5 & -2 & -1 & -2 \end{array}\right] = -279 \]
