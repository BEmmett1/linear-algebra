Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 5 & -3 & 0 & 1 \\ 3 & -5 & -1 & -1 \\ 5 & -5 & 3 & 4 \\ -2 & -1 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 5 & -3 & 0 & 1 \\ 3 & -5 & -1 & -1 \\ 5 & -5 & 3 & 4 \\ -2 & -1 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] = -43 \]
