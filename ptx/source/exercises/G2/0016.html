Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 6 & -1 & -1 & 1 \\ -5 & -3 & 0 & -5 \\ 0 & 6 & 2 & -2 \\ 5 & 5 & 0 & -3 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 6 & -1 & -1 & 1 \\ -5 & -3 & 0 & -5 \\ 0 & 6 & 2 & -2 \\ 5 & 5 & 0 & -3 \end{array}\right] = 248 \]
