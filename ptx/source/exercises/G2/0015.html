Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -6 & 2 & 6 & 0 \\ 2 & 2 & -6 & 0 \\ -6 & -3 & 0 & 1 \\ 4 & -4 & -3 & -3 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -6 & 2 & 6 & 0 \\ 2 & 2 & -6 & 0 \\ -6 & -3 & 0 & 1 \\ 4 & -4 & -3 & -3 \end{array}\right] = -696 \]
