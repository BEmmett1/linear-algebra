Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 2 & 6 & 3 & -2 \\ 6 & -4 & 2 & -3 \\ -2 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ -4 & 5 & 6 & -1 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 2 & 6 & 3 & -2 \\ 6 & -4 & 2 & -3 \\ -2 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ -4 & 5 & 6 & -1 \end{array}\right] = -214 \]
