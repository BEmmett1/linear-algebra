Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & 1 & 1 & 6 \\ -1 & 0 & 5 & -3 \\ -4 & -2 & 5 & -6 \\ -6 & 0 & 2 & -2 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & 1 & 1 & 6 \\ -1 & 0 & 5 & -3 \\ -4 & -2 & 5 & -6 \\ -6 & 0 & 2 & -2 \end{array}\right] = 296 \]
