Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -6 & -5 & 2 & 4 \\ 0 & -1 & 0 & 3 \\ -6 & 1 & 1 & 3 \\ 3 & 5 & -1 & 6 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -6 & -5 & 2 & 4 \\ 0 & -1 & 0 & 3 \\ -6 & 1 & 1 & 3 \\ 3 & 5 & -1 & 6 \end{array}\right] = -93 \]
