Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 2 & 3 & 3 & -5 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & -2 \\ 6 & 6 & 1 & 3 \\ 4 & 3 & 2 & -6 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 2 & 3 & 3 & -5 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & -2 \\ 6 & 6 & 1 & 3 \\ 4 & 3 & 2 & -6 \end{array}\right] = 132 \]
