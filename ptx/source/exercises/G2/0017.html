Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 5 & 4 & -3 & 4 \\ -2 & 0 & -5 & 3 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ -1 & -6 & -6 & 2 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 5 & 4 & -3 & 4 \\ -2 & 0 & -5 & 3 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ -1 & -6 & -6 & 2 \end{array}\right] = 608 \]
