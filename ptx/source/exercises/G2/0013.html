Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix

\[A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & -6 & -2 & -3 \\ 1 & 4 & -6 & -6 \\ -2 & 2 & -6 & -6 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & -2 \end{array}\right] .\]

.

\[\operatorname{det}\ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & -6 & -2 & -3 \\ 1 & 4 & -6 & -6 \\ -2 & 2 & -6 & -6 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & -2 \end{array}\right] = 118 \]
