Explain why the matrix \(A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 2 & 0 & -3 & -4 \\ 3 & 1 & 0 & -3 \\ 3 & 1 & 1 & -3 \\ 1 & 0 & -4 & -2 \end{array}\right] \) is or is not invertible.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 2 & 0 & -3 & -4 \\ 3 & 1 & 0 & -3 \\ 3 & 1 & 1 & -3 \\ 1 & 0 & -4 & -2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 0 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

\(A\) is not invertible.
