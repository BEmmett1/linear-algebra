Explain why the matrix \(P= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 3 & 1 & -8 & 3 \\ -1 & 0 & 1 & -2 \\ -1 & -1 & 7 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -4 & -1 \end{array}\right] \) is or is not invertible.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 3 & 1 & -8 & 3 \\ -1 & 0 & 1 & -2 \\ -1 & -1 & 7 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -4 & -1 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

\(P\) is not invertible.
