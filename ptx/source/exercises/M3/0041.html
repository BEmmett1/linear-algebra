Explain why the matrix \(A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & -3 & -6 & 8 \\ -1 & 2 & 5 & -8 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -3 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & -3 \end{array}\right] \) is or is not invertible.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & -3 & -6 & 8 \\ -1 & 2 & 5 & -8 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -3 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & -3 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 0 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

\(A\) is not invertible.
