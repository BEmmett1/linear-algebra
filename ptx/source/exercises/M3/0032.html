Explain why the matrix \(C= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -3 & -4 & -4 & -3 \\ 5 & 6 & 5 & 2 \\ 4 & 5 & 5 & 3 \\ -3 & -4 & -4 & -3 \end{array}\right] \) is or is not invertible.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -3 & -4 & -4 & -3 \\ 5 & 6 & 5 & 2 \\ 4 & 5 & 5 & 3 \\ -3 & -4 & -4 & -3 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 0 & 0 & -3 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

\(C\) is not invertible.
