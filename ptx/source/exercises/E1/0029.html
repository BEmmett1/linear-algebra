Consider the augmented matrix

\[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -3 & -1 & 7 & 1 \\ -2 & -1 & 5 & 1 \\ 1 & -2 & 0 & 2 \\ -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this augmented matrix.
  2. Write a vector equation corresponding to this augmented matrix.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} -3 \, x_{1} &-& x_{2} &+& 7 \, x_{3} &=& 1 \\-2 \, x_{1} &-& x_{2} &+& 5 \, x_{3} &=& 1 \\x_{1} &-& 2 \, x_{2} & & &=& 2 \\-x_{1} & & &+& 2 \, x_{3} &=& 0 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -3 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -1 \\ -2 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 7 \\ 5 \\ 0 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] \]
