Consider the augmented matrix

\[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 0 & 0 & -1 & -2 \\ -3 & -2 & 2 & 8 \\ 1 & 1 & -1 & -3 \\ 0 & 2 & -4 & -6 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this augmented matrix.
  2. Write a vector equation corresponding to this augmented matrix.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} & & &-& z &=& -2 \\-3 \, x &-& 2 \, y &+& 2 \, z &=& 8 \\x &+& y &-& z &=& -3 \\ & & 2 \, y &-& 4 \, z &=& -6 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 2 \\ -1 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 8 \\ -3 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] \]
