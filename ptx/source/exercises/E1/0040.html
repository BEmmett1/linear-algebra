Consider the system of equations

\begin{alignat*}{4} -2 \, x_{1} &+& 3 \, x_{2} &+& 3 \, x_{3} &=& 6 \\-3 \, x_{1} &+& 4 \, x_{2} &+& 6 \, x_{3} &=& 7 \\-x_{1} & & &+& 6 \, x_{3} &=& -2 \\x_{1} &-& x_{2} &-& 3 \, x_{3} &=& 3 \\ \end{alignat*}

  1. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this system.
  2. Write a vector equation corresponding to this system.

Answer:

  1. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -2 & 3 & 3 & 6 \\ -3 & 4 & 6 & 7 \\ -1 & 0 & 6 & -2 \\ 1 & -1 & -3 & 3 \end{array}\right] \]

  2. \[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -3 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 4 \\ 0 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 6 \\ 6 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 7 \\ -2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] \]
