Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -1 \\ 2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 5 \\ -5 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} -x_{1} &-& 2 \, x_{2} & & &=& 2 \\-x_{1} &-& 3 \, x_{2} &-& x_{3} &=& 5 \\ & & x_{2} &+& 2 \, x_{3} &=& -5 \\ &-& x_{2} &+& 2 \, x_{3} &=& -3 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & -2 & 0 & 2 \\ -1 & -3 & -1 & 5 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & -5 \\ 0 & -1 & 2 & -3 \end{array}\right] \]
