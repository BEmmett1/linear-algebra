Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -1 \\ -2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 5 \\ -2 \\ -5 \\ 8 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} 2 \, x &+& 5 \, y & & &=& 0 \\-x &-& 2 \, y &+& z &=& 0 \\-2 \, x &-& 5 \, y &+& z &=& 0 \\2 \, x &+& 8 \, y &+& 4 \, z &=& 0 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 2 & 5 & 0 & 0 \\ -1 & -2 & 1 & 0 \\ -2 & -5 & 1 & 0 \\ 2 & 8 & 4 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]
