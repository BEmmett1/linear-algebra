Consider the system of equations

\begin{alignat*}{4} -x &+& y &+& 4 \, z &=& -4 \\-x &-& 2 \, y &-& 5 \, z &=& 2 \\ &-& y &-& 3 \, z &=& 2 \\-x &-& y &-& 2 \, z &=& 0 \\ \end{alignat*}

  1. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this system.
  2. Write a vector equation corresponding to this system.

Answer:

  1. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 1 & 4 & -4 \\ -1 & -2 & -5 & 2 \\ 0 & -1 & -3 & 2 \\ -1 & -1 & -2 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  2. \[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ -2 \\ -1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 4 \\ -5 \\ -3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -4 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] \]
