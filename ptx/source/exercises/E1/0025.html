Consider the system of equations

\begin{alignat*}{4} x &+& 2 \, y & & &=& 6 \\ & & y &+& z &=& 3 \\x &-& y &-& 2 \, z &=& -2 \\ & & 2 \, y &-& z &=& 3 \\ \end{alignat*}

  1. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this system.
  2. Write a vector equation corresponding to this system.

Answer:

  1. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 2 & 0 & 6 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & 3 \\ 1 & -1 & -2 & -2 \\ 0 & 2 & -1 & 3 \end{array}\right] \]

  2. \[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \\ -2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] \]
