Consider the augmented matrix

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 4 & -7 & 2 & 8 & -3 \\ -1 & 5 & -7 & -2 & 4 \\ -1 & 1 & 1 & -2 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this augmented matrix.
  2. Write a vector equation corresponding to this augmented matrix.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{5} 4 \, x_{1} &-& 7 \, x_{2} &+& 2 \, x_{3} &+& 8 \, x_{4} &=& -3 \\-x_{1} &+& 5 \, x_{2} &-& 7 \, x_{3} &-& 2 \, x_{4} &=& 4 \\-x_{1} &+& x_{2} &+& x_{3} &-& 2 \, x_{4} &=& 0 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 4 \\ -1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -7 \\ 5 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -7 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] + x_{4} \left[\begin{array}{c} 8 \\ -2 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -3 \\ 4 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] \]
