Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 8 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ -7 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} -2 \, x &+& y &+& 8 \, z &=& 2 \\-x & & &+& 3 \, z &=& 3 \\ & & & & 0 &=& 1 \\2 \, x & & &-& 6 \, z &=& -7 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -2 & 1 & 8 & 2 \\ -1 & 0 & 3 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\ 2 & 0 & -6 & -7 \end{array}\right] \]
