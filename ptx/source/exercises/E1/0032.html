Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 2 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} -7 \\ 7 \\ 2 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -3 \\ 3 \\ 2 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} x &-& 2 \, y &-& 7 \, z &=& -3 \\-x &+& 2 \, y &+& 7 \, z &=& 3 \\ & & y &+& 2 \, z &=& 2 \\2 \, x &-& y &-& 8 \, z &=& 0 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & -2 & -7 & -3 \\ -1 & 2 & 7 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 2 \\ 2 & -1 & -8 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]
