Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] + x_{4} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 0 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 5 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{5} -x & & & & &-& {w} &=& 2 \\-x &-& y &+& 2 \, z & & &=& 5 \\ & & 2 \, y &-& 4 \, z &-& 2 \, {w} &=& -6 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} -1 & 0 & 0 & -1 & 2 \\ -1 & -1 & 2 & 0 & 5 \\ 0 & 2 & -4 & -2 & -6 \end{array}\right] \]
