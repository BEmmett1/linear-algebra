Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 3 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} -4 \\ -7 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] + x_{4} \left[\begin{array}{c} 4 \\ 4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 6 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{5} x &-& y &-& 4 \, z &+& 4 \, {w} &=& 3 \\3 \, x &-& 2 \, y &-& 7 \, z &+& 4 \, {w} &=& 6 \\x &-& y &-& 3 \, z &+& 2 \, {w} &=& 3 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -1 & -4 & 4 & 3 \\ 3 & -2 & -7 & 4 & 6 \\ 1 & -1 & -3 & 2 & 3 \end{array}\right] \]
