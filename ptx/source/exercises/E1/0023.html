Consider the augmented matrix

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 0 & -1 & -2 & 0 & -1 \\ 1 & -1 & 1 & 0 & -3 \\ 0 & -3 & -6 & 0 & -3 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this augmented matrix.
  2. Write a vector equation corresponding to this augmented matrix.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{5} &-& y &-& 2 \, z & & &=& -1 \\x &-& y &+& z & & &=& -3 \\ &-& 3 \, y &-& 6 \, z & & &=& -3 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -1 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 1 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] + x_{4} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] \]
