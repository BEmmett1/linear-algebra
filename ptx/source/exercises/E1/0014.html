Consider the augmented matrix

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} -2 & 6 & -4 & 0 & -6 \\ 0 & 1 & -1 & 1 & -1 \\ -1 & 1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this augmented matrix.
  2. Write a vector equation corresponding to this augmented matrix.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{5} -2 \, x_{1} &+& 6 \, x_{2} &-& 4 \, x_{3} & & &=& -6 \\ & & x_{2} &-& x_{3} &+& x_{4} &=& -1 \\-x_{1} &+& x_{2} & & &-& 2 \, x_{4} &=& -1 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 0 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} -4 \\ -1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{4} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -6 \\ -1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] \]
