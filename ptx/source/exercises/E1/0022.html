Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ -4 \\ -2 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 5 \\ 1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 5 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ 8 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -5 \\ -1 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} 3 \, x_{1} &-& 2 \, x_{2} &+& 5 \, x_{3} &=& 2 \\-4 \, x_{1} &+& 5 \, x_{2} &-& x_{3} &=& -5 \\-2 \, x_{1} &+& x_{2} &-& 4 \, x_{3} &=& -1 \\ & & 3 \, x_{2} &+& 8 \, x_{3} &=& -3 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 3 & -2 & 5 & 2 \\ -4 & 5 & -1 & -5 \\ -2 & 1 & -4 & -1 \\ 0 & 3 & 8 & -3 \end{array}\right] \]
