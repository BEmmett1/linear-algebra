Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 4 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] + x_{4} \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ -5 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{5} &-& y &+& 3 \, z &+& 3 \, {w} &=& -2 \\x & & &+& 4 \, z &-& 5 \, {w} &=& 2 \\x & & &+& 4 \, z &-& 4 \, {w} &=& 2 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 0 & -1 & 3 & 3 & -2 \\ 1 & 0 & 4 & -5 & 2 \\ 1 & 0 & 4 & -4 & 2 \end{array}\right] \]
