Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -2 \\ -2 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 6 \\ 6 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -5 \\ -8 \\ -8 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} -x &+& 3 \, y &+& 2 \, z &=& -5 \\-2 \, x &+& 6 \, y &+& 3 \, z &=& -8 \\-2 \, x &+& 6 \, y &+& 3 \, z &=& -8 \\ & & & & 4 \, z &=& -8 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 3 & 2 & -5 \\ -2 & 6 & 3 & -8 \\ -2 & 6 & 3 & -8 \\ 0 & 0 & 4 & -8 \end{array}\right] \]
