Consider the system of equations

\begin{alignat*}{4} x &-& 2 \, y &-& z &=& 0 \\ & & y &+& z &=& 0 \\-2 \, x &+& 7 \, y &+& 5 \, z &=& 0 \\2 \, x &-& 5 \, y &-& 3 \, z &=& 0 \\ \end{alignat*}

  1. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this system.
  2. Write a vector equation corresponding to this system.

Answer:

  1. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & -2 & -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & 0 \\ -2 & 7 & 5 & 0 \\ 2 & -5 & -3 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  2. \[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 1 \\ 7 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 1 \\ 5 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] \]
