Consider the augmented matrix

\[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 1 & 3 & -5 \\ -1 & 0 & -2 & 0 \\ -1 & 0 & -1 & -1 \\ 2 & 0 & 6 & -2 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this augmented matrix.
  2. Write a vector equation corresponding to this augmented matrix.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} -x &+& y &+& 3 \, z &=& -5 \\-x & & &-& 2 \, z &=& 0 \\-x & & &-& z &=& -1 \\2 \, x & & &+& 6 \, z &=& -2 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ -2 \\ -1 \\ 6 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -5 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] \]
