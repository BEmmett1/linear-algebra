Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -2 \\ 0 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} -x &-& y & & &=& 0 \\-2 \, x &-& 3 \, y &+& z &=& -2 \\ &-& 2 \, y &+& 2 \, z &=& -4 \\-5 \, x &-& 8 \, y &+& 3 \, z &=& -6 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & -1 & 0 & 0 \\ -2 & -3 & 1 & -2 \\ 0 & -2 & 2 & -4 \\ -5 & -8 & 3 & -6 \end{array}\right] \]
