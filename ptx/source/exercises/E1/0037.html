Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} -4 \\ 3 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} -2 \, x & & &-& 4 \, z &=& -2 \\ & & y &+& 3 \, z &=& -2 \\x & & &+& 2 \, z &=& 1 \\-x & & &-& 2 \, z &=& -1 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -2 & 0 & -4 & -2 \\ 0 & 1 & 3 & -2 \\ 1 & 0 & 2 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \end{array}\right] \]
