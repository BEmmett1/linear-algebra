Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 5 \\ -1 \\ -7 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -4 \\ 0 \\ 7 \\ 6 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{4} x &+& y &+& 5 \, z &=& -4 \\-x & & &-& z &=& 0 \\ &-& 2 \, y &-& 7 \, z &=& 7 \\ &-& 2 \, y &-& 6 \, z &=& 6 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 1 & 5 & -4 \\ -1 & 0 & -1 & 0 \\ 0 & -2 & -7 & 7 \\ 0 & -2 & -6 & 6 \end{array}\right] \]
