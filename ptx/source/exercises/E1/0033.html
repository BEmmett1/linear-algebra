Consider the vector equation.

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ -1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -3 \\ 3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] + x_{4} \left[\begin{array}{c} 5 \\ -4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ -2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this vector equation.
  2. Write an augmented matrix corresponding to this vector equation.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{5} x_{1} &-& 3 \, x_{2} &+& x_{3} &+& 5 \, x_{4} &=& 3 \\-x_{1} &+& 3 \, x_{2} &-& x_{3} &-& 4 \, x_{4} &=& -2 \\ & & &-& x_{3} &-& 3 \, x_{4} &=& -1 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -3 & 1 & 5 & 3 \\ -1 & 3 & -1 & -4 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & -3 & -1 \end{array}\right] \]
