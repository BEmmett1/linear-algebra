Explain how to find a basis for the eigenspace associated to the eigenvalue \( 2 \) in the matrix

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -1 & 2 & 2 & -7 \\ -1 & 3 & -1 & -3 \\ -4 & 2 & 7 & -8 \\ 4 & -2 & -5 & 10 \end{array}\right] \]

Answer:

\[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -3 & 2 & 2 & -7 \\ -1 & 1 & -1 & -3 \\ -4 & 2 & 5 & -8 \\ 4 & -2 & -5 & 8 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

A basis of the eigenspace is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 2 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] \right\} \).
