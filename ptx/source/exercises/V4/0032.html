\begin{align*} \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} x \\ y \\ z \\ w \end{array}\right] \middle|\,w + 7 \, x = 2 \, y + 4 \, z\right\} & & \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} x \\ y \\ z \\ w \end{array}\right] \middle|\,x^{2} + 7 \, y + 5 \, z = 5 \, w\right\} \\ \end{align*}

Explain why one of these sets is a subspace of \(\mathbb{R}^ 4 \) and one is not.
