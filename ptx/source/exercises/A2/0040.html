1. Find the standard matrix for the linear transformation \(S:\mathbb{R}^ 3 \to \mathbb{R}^ 3 \) given by

    \[S\left( \left[\begin{array}{c} x_{1} \\ x_{2} \\ x_{3} \end{array}\right] \right) = \left[\begin{array}{c} 5 \, x_{1} - 7 \, x_{2} + 2 \, x_{3} \\ 4 \, x_{1} - 7 \, x_{2} + 5 \, x_{3} \\ 4 \, x_{1} - 5 \, x_{2} \end{array}\right] .\]

  2. Let \(T:\mathbb{R}^ 4 \to \mathbb{R}^ 1 \) be the linear transformation given by the standard matrix

    \[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 1 & 0 & -2 \end{array}\right] .\]

    Compute \(T\left( \left[\begin{array}{c} -5 \\ -2 \\ -2 \\ 7 \end{array}\right] \right)\).

Answer:

  1. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc} 5 & -7 & 2 \\ 4 & -7 & 5 \\ 4 & -5 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  2. \[T\left( \left[\begin{array}{c} -5 \\ -2 \\ -2 \\ 7 \end{array}\right] \right)= \left[\begin{array}{c} -21 \end{array}\right] \]
