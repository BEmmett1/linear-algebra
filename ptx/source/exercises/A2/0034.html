1. Find the standard matrix for the linear transformation \(S:\mathbb{R}^ 4 \to \mathbb{R}^ 3 \) given by

    \[S\left( \left[\begin{array}{c} x \\ y \\ z \\ {w} \end{array}\right] \right) = \left[\begin{array}{c} -z - {w} \\ -3 \, x + y - 5 \, z + 8 \, {w} \\ x + 3 \, z \end{array}\right] .\]

  2. Let \(T:\mathbb{R}^ 3 \to \mathbb{R}^ 3 \) be the linear transformation given by the standard matrix

    \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc} 1 & 1 & -6 \\ 1 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & -1 & 6 \end{array}\right] .\]

    Compute \(T\left( \left[\begin{array}{c} 8 \\ 6 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] \right)\).

Answer:

  1. \[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & 0 & -1 & -1 \\ -3 & 1 & -5 & 8 \\ 1 & 0 & 3 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  2. \[T\left( \left[\begin{array}{c} 8 \\ 6 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] \right)= \left[\begin{array}{c} 8 \\ 9 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] \]
