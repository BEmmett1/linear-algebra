1. Find the standard matrix for the linear transformation \(S:\mathbb{R}^ 3 \to \mathbb{R}^ 3 \) given by

    \[S\left( \left[\begin{array}{c} x \\ y \\ z \end{array}\right] \right) = \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \, x + y \\ x + y + 2 \, z \\ -3 \, x - 2 \, y - z \end{array}\right] .\]

  2. Let \(T:\mathbb{R}^ 4 \to \mathbb{R}^ 3 \) be the linear transformation given by the standard matrix

    \[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & -3 & 2 & 0 \\ -1 & 4 & -4 & -1 \\ -3 & 8 & -4 & 2 \end{array}\right] .\]

    Compute \(T\left( \left[\begin{array}{c} 8 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] \right)\).

Answer:

  1. \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc} 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 & 2 \\ -3 & -2 & -1 \end{array}\right] \]

  2. \[T\left( \left[\begin{array}{c} 8 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] \right)= \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] \]
