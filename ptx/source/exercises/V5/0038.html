Consider the statement

The set of vectors \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -6 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ -5 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 5 \\ -6 \\ -6 \\ 5 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 23 \\ 3 \\ -12 \\ 20 \\ -9 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -7 \\ 12 \\ -6 \\ 14 \\ -12 \end{array}\right] \right\} \)is linearly dependent.

  1. Write an equivalent statement using a vector equation.
  2. Explain why your statement is true or false.

  1. The statement
