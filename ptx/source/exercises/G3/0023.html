Explain how to find the eigenvalues of the matrix \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -2 & 1 \\ -6 & -7 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

The characteristic polynomial of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -2 & 1 \\ -6 & -7 \end{array}\right] \) is \( \lambda^{2} + 9 \lambda + 20 \).

The eigenvalues of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -2 & 1 \\ -6 & -7 \end{array}\right] \) are \( -5 \) and \( -4 \).
