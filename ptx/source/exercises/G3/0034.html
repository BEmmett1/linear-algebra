Explain how to find the eigenvalues of the matrix \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 11 & 2 \\ -30 & -5 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

The characteristic polynomial of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 11 & 2 \\ -30 & -5 \end{array}\right] \) is \( \lambda^{2} - 6 \lambda + 5 \).

The eigenvalues of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 11 & 2 \\ -30 & -5 \end{array}\right] \) are \( 5 \) and \( 1 \).
