Explain how to find the eigenvalues of the matrix \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -3 & 1 \\ 8 & 4 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

The characteristic polynomial of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -3 & 1 \\ 8 & 4 \end{array}\right] \) is \( \lambda^{2} - \lambda - 20 \).

The eigenvalues of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -3 & 1 \\ 8 & 4 \end{array}\right] \) are \( -4 \) and \( 5 \).
