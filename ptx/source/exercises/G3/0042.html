Explain how to find the eigenvalues of the matrix \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 3 & 2 \\ -7 & -6 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

The characteristic polynomial of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 3 & 2 \\ -7 & -6 \end{array}\right] \) is \( \lambda^{2} + 3 \lambda - 4 \).

The eigenvalues of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 3 & 2 \\ -7 & -6 \end{array}\right] \) are \( 1 \) and \( -4 \).
