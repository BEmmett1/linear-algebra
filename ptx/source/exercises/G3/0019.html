Explain how to find the eigenvalues of the matrix \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 8 & 2 \\ -6 & 0 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

The characteristic polynomial of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 8 & 2 \\ -6 & 0 \end{array}\right] \) is \( \lambda^{2} - 8 \lambda + 12 \).

The eigenvalues of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 8 & 2 \\ -6 & 0 \end{array}\right] \) are \( 2 \) and \( 6 \).
