Explain how to find the eigenvalues of the matrix \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & 1 \\ 2 & -4 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

The characteristic polynomial of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & 1 \\ 2 & -4 \end{array}\right] \) is \( \lambda^{2} + 9 \lambda + 18 \).

The eigenvalues of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & 1 \\ 2 & -4 \end{array}\right] \) are \( -6 \) and \( -3 \).
