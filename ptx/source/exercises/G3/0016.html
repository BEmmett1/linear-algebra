Explain how to find the eigenvalues of the matrix \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

The characteristic polynomial of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array}\right] \) is \( \lambda^{2} - 6 \lambda + 8 \).

The eigenvalues of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array}\right] \) are \( 2 \) and \( 4 \).
