Explain how to find the eigenvalues of the matrix \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & 2 \\ 9 & 3 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

The characteristic polynomial of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & 2 \\ 9 & 3 \end{array}\right] \) is \( \lambda^{2} + \lambda - 30 \).

The eigenvalues of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & 2 \\ 9 & 3 \end{array}\right] \) are \( -6 \) and \( 5 \).
