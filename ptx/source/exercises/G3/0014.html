Explain how to find the eigenvalues of the matrix \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & 1 \\ 0 & -6 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

The characteristic polynomial of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & 1 \\ 0 & -6 \end{array}\right] \) is \( \lambda^{2} + 10 \lambda + 24 \).

The eigenvalues of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & 1 \\ 0 & -6 \end{array}\right] \) are \( -6 \) and \( -4 \).
