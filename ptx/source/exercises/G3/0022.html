Explain how to find the eigenvalues of the matrix \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 6 & 1 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

The characteristic polynomial of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 6 & 1 \end{array}\right] \) is \( \lambda^{2} - \lambda - 6 \).

The eigenvalues of \( \left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 6 & 1 \end{array}\right] \) are \( -2 \) and \( 3 \).
