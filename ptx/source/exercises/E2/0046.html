1. Show that

    \[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -5 & -4 & -1 & 2 \\ 4 & 9 & -5 & 10 \\ 5 & 8 & -3 & 6 \\ -2 & -2 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 0 & 1 & -2 \\ 0 & 1 & -1 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] .\]

  2. Explain why the matrix \(B= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -3 & 6 & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \) is or is not in reduced row echelon form.

Answer:

  1. \(\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -5 & -4 & -1 & 2 \\ 4 & 9 & -5 & 10 \\ 5 & 8 & -3 & 6 \\ -2 & -2 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 0 & 1 & -2 \\ 0 & 1 & -1 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] .\)
  2. \(B\) is not in reduced row echelon form because the pivots are not all \(1\).
