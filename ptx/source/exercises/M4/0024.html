Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(B= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -2 & -1 & -3 & -4 \\ 3 & 1 & 6 & 5 \\ 1 & -1 & 7 & -2 \\ 2 & -2 & 7 & 4 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[B^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 125 & 94 & -23 & -4 \\ -43 & -32 & 8 & 1 \\ -32 & -24 & 6 & 1 \\ -28 & -21 & 5 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
