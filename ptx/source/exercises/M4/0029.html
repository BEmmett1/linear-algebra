Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(N= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 1 & 2 & -1 \\ -3 & -2 & -8 & 7 \\ 1 & 0 & 5 & -5 \\ 3 & 4 & 1 & 2 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[N^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -10 & 5 & 11 & 5 \\ 7 & -5 & -10 & -4 \\ 2 & 1 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & 3 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
