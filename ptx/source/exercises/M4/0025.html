Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(P= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 2 & -2 & 3 \\ -3 & -5 & 5 & -7 \\ 2 & 2 & -1 & 3 \\ -3 & -5 & 4 & -7 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[P^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -1 & -1 & 1 & 1 \\ -5 & 0 & -2 & -3 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -1 \\ 4 & 1 & 1 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
