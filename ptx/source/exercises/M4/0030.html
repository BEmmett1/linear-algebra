Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(M= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & -2 & 1 & -3 \\ 0 & 1 & -1 & 3 \\ -2 & 5 & -2 & 4 \\ -2 & 3 & -1 & 4 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[M^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 7 & 3 & 1 & 2 \\ 6 & 2 & 1 & 2 \\ 12 & 4 & 1 & 5 \\ 2 & 1 & 0 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
