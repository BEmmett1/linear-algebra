Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(Q= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & -2 & 2 & -2 \\ 2 & -3 & 2 & -6 \\ -3 & 7 & -7 & 7 \\ 0 & 4 & -8 & -7 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[Q^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 7 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ -24 & 15 & 2 & -4 \\ -19 & 11 & 1 & -3 \\ 8 & -4 & 0 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
