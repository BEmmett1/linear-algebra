Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(P= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 3 & 0 & -2 & -2 \\ 1 & -1 & -2 & -2 \\ -1 & -1 & -1 & -2 \\ -2 & 2 & 4 & 5 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[P^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -1 & -2 & -2 & -2 \\ 3 & 3 & 4 & 4 \\ -2 & -5 & -3 & -4 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
