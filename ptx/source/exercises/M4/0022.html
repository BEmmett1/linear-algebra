Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(P= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 3 & 7 & 4 & 1 \\ -1 & 2 & -2 & 6 \\ -1 & -4 & -1 & -3 \\ -2 & 6 & -1 & 6 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[P^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 30 & 29 & 64 & -2 \\ 21 & 20 & 45 & -1 \\ -54 & -52 & -116 & 3 \\ -20 & -19 & -43 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
