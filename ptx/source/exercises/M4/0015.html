Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(C= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & -1 & -5 & 8 \\ 1 & 1 & 4 & -1 \\ -2 & -1 & -2 & -8 \\ 0 & 0 & -3 & 7 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[C^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -13 & -27 & -14 & -5 \\ -12 & -22 & -11 & -2 \\ 7 & 14 & 7 & 2 \\ 3 & 6 & 3 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
