Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(B= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -1 & -1 & 2 & 2 \\ 1 & 0 & -2 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 & 3 & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[B^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & 0 & -1 & 3 \\ -1 & -4 & -3 & 3 \\ 0 & -1 & -1 & 2 \\ 0 & -1 & -1 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
