Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(Q= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & -1 & -1 & 3 \\ 2 & 1 & -2 & 6 \\ -1 & -1 & 1 & -4 \\ 1 & 0 & -2 & 8 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[Q^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -4 & 2 & 6 & 3 \\ 2 & -1 & -4 & -2 \\ -6 & 1 & 7 & 5 \\ -1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
