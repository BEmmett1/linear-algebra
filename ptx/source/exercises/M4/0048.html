Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -3 & -2 & -3 & 5 \\ 3 & 4 & -5 & 8 \\ 3 & 4 & -4 & 7 \\ 4 & 3 & 3 & -5 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[A^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -17 & 1 & 1 & -14 \\ 18 & -1 & -1 & 15 \\ -7 & -2 & 3 & -6 \\ -7 & -1 & 2 & -6 \end{array}\right] \]
