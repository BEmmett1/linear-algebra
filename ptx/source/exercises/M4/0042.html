Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(M= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & -4 & -6 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 & 0 \\ 0 & -5 & -4 & 7 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[M^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -13 & 56 & 14 & 16 \\ -8 & 35 & 8 & 10 \\ 3 & -14 & -3 & -4 \\ -4 & 17 & 4 & 5 \end{array}\right] \]
