Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(Q= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & -1 & 2 & 4 \\ 0 & 1 & -5 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ -1 & 0 & 1 & -4 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[Q^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -4 & -4 & -7 & -5 \\ -1 & 0 & 3 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 & 2 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
