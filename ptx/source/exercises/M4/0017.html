Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(B= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 2 & -4 & 0 \\ -2 & -3 & 7 & 0 \\ 3 & 1 & -6 & 2 \\ -1 & -4 & 7 & 3 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[B^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 19 & 20 & 6 & -4 \\ 13 & 12 & 3 & -2 \\ 11 & 11 & 3 & -2 \\ -2 & -3 & -1 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
