Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(A= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -3 & 0 & 1 & -2 \\ -4 & 1 & 1 & -8 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ -2 & 0 & 1 & -3 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[A^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 5 & 1 & 7 & -6 \\ 3 & 0 & 5 & -2 \\ 1 & 0 & 1 & -1 \end{array}\right] \]
