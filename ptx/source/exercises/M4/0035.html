Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(M= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 2 & -5 & 5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -2 & -3 \\ -1 & 2 & -1 & 3 \\ -2 & 5 & -8 & -8 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[M^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 3 & 5 & 5 & 0 \\ -10 & -6 & -14 & -3 \\ -11 & -8 & -16 & -3 \\ 4 & 3 & 6 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
