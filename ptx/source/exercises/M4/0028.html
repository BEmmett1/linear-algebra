Show how to find the inverse of the matrix \(N= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -1 & 1 & 2 & -6 \\ -2 & -3 & 1 & -3 \\ 2 & 1 & -2 & 6 \\ 3 & 5 & 0 & 1 \end{array}\right] \).

Answer:

\[N^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 5 & 4 & 7 & 0 \\ -2 & -2 & -3 & 0 \\ -11 & -3 & -13 & 3 \\ -5 & -2 & -6 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]
