1 & -2 \\
3 & -5 \\

\end{array}\right]  & & C= \left[\begin{array}{ccc}

-3 & 1 & 1 \\
2 & 0 & -3 \\
0 & -1 & 2
\end{array}\right]  \\ \end{align*}
             Explain which two can be multiplied and why. Then show how to find their product.
