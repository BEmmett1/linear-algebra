Consider the homogeneous system of equations

\begin{alignat*}{6} -2 \, x_{1} &+& x_{2} &-& 11 \, x_{3} &+& 6 \, x_{4} &-& 2 \, x_{5} &=& 0 \\x_{1} &-& x_{2} &+& 7 \, x_{3} &-& x_{4} & & &=& 0 \\x_{1} &-& x_{2} &+& 7 \, x_{3} & & & & &=& 0 \\ \end{alignat*}

  1. Find the solution space of this system.
  2. Find a basis of the solution space.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{ccccc|c} -2 & 1 & -11 & 6 & -2 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & 7 & -1 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & 7 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{ccccc|c} 1 & 0 & 4 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. The solution space is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -4 \, a - 2 \, b \\ 3 \, a - 2 \, b \\ a \\ 0 \\ b \end{array}\right] \middle|\,a\text{\texttt{,}}b\in\mathbb{R}\right\} \)
  2. A basis of the solution space is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -4 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -2 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] \right\} \).
