Consider the homogeneous system of equations

\begin{alignat*}{6} 3 \, x_{1} &-& 9 \, x_{2} &-& 7 \, x_{3} &-& 8 \, x_{4} &-& 11 \, x_{5} &=& 0 \\2 \, x_{1} &-& 6 \, x_{2} &-& 7 \, x_{3} &-& 10 \, x_{4} &-& 12 \, x_{5} &=& 0 \\2 \, x_{1} &-& 6 \, x_{2} &-& 3 \, x_{3} &-& 2 \, x_{4} &-& 4 \, x_{5} &=& 0 \\ \end{alignat*}

  1. Find the solution space of this system.
  2. Find a basis of the solution space.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{ccccc|c} 3 & -9 & -7 & -8 & -11 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & -7 & -10 & -12 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & -3 & -2 & -4 & 0 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{ccccc|c} 1 & -3 & 0 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 2 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. The solution space is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \, a - 2 \, b - c \\ a \\ -2 \, b - 2 \, c \\ b \\ c \end{array}\right] \middle|\,a\text{\texttt{,}}b\text{\texttt{,}}c\in\mathbb{R}\right\} \)
  2. A basis of the solution space is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] \right\} \).
