Consider the homogeneous system of equations

\begin{alignat*}{6} &-& x_{2} &-& 2 \, x_{3} & & &-& x_{5} &=& 0 \\x_{1} &-& x_{2} &+& x_{3} & & &-& 3 \, x_{5} &=& 0 \\ &-& 3 \, x_{2} &-& 6 \, x_{3} & & &-& 3 \, x_{5} &=& 0 \\ \end{alignat*}

  1. Find the solution space of this system.
  2. Find a basis of the solution space.

Answer:

\[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{ccccc|c} 0 & -1 & -2 & 0 & -1 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & 1 & 0 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & -3 & -6 & 0 & -3 & 0 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{ccccc|c} 1 & 0 & 3 & 0 & -2 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. The solution space is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -3 \, a + 2 \, c \\ -2 \, a - c \\ a \\ b \\ c \end{array}\right] \middle|\,a\text{\texttt{,}}b\text{\texttt{,}}c\in\mathbb{R}\right\} \)
  2. A basis of the solution space is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -3 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] \right\} \).
