Let \(A\) be a \(4 \times 4\) matrix with determinant \( 2 \).

  1. Let \(M\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_3 \leftrightarrow R_4 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ M\)?
  2. Let \(N\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_4 \to R_4 + -5R_1 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ N\)?
  3. Let \(B\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_3 \to -4R_3 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ B\)?

Answer:

  1. \(\operatorname{det}\ M= -2 \)
  2. \(\operatorname{det}\ N= 2 \)
  3. \(\operatorname{det}\ B= -8 \)
