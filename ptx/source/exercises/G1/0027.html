Let \(A\) be a \(4 \times 4\) matrix with determinant \( 3 \).

  1. Let \(M\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_1 \to R_1 + 3R_2 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ M\)?
  2. Let \(P\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_2 \to -4R_2 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ P\)?
  3. Let \(B\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_1 \leftrightarrow R_4 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ B\)?

Answer:

  1. \(\operatorname{det}\ M= 3 \)
  2. \(\operatorname{det}\ P= -12 \)
  3. \(\operatorname{det}\ B= -3 \)
