Let \(A\) be a \(4 \times 4\) matrix with determinant \( -3 \).

  1. Let \(M\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_2 \leftrightarrow R_1 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ M\)?
  2. Let \(Q\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_1 \to -2R_1 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ Q\)?
  3. Let \(C\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_3 \to R_3 + -2R_4 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ C\)?

Answer:

  1. \(\operatorname{det}\ M= 3 \)
  2. \(\operatorname{det}\ Q= 6 \)
  3. \(\operatorname{det}\ C= -3 \)
