Let \(A\) be a \(4 \times 4\) matrix with determinant \( -4 \).

  1. Let \(C\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_4 \to R_4 + 5R_3 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ C\)?
  2. Let \(B\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_3 \to 4R_3 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ B\)?
  3. Let \(N\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_1 \leftrightarrow R_4 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ N\)?

Answer:

  1. \(\operatorname{det}\ C= -4 \)
  2. \(\operatorname{det}\ B= -16 \)
  3. \(\operatorname{det}\ N= 4 \)
