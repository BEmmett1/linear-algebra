Let \(A\) be a \(4 \times 4\) matrix with determinant \( -5 \).

  1. Let \(Q\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_3 \leftrightarrow R_1 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ Q\)?
  2. Let \(N\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_1 \to R_1 + -3R_2 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ N\)?
  3. Let \(M\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_1 \to -2R_1 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ M\)?

Answer:

  1. \(\operatorname{det}\ Q= 5 \)
  2. \(\operatorname{det}\ N= -5 \)
  3. \(\operatorname{det}\ M= 10 \)
