2
\end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c}
1 \\
1 \\
2 \\
-3
\end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c}
-1 \\
1 \\
-6 \\
-4
\end{array}\right] .\]


\end{exerciseStatement}
