Show how to find the solution set for the following vector equation

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ -3 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ -6 \\ 7 \end{array}\right] + x_{4} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -4 \\ 6 \end{array}\right] .\]

Answer:

\[\mathrm{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 0 & 1 & 1 & 0 & 2 \\ -1 & -3 & -6 & 0 & -4 \\ 1 & 4 & 7 & 0 & 6 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & 3 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

The solution set is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -3 \, a - 2 \\ -a + 2 \\ a \\ b \end{array}\right] \middle|\,a\text{\texttt{,}}b\in\mathbb{R}\right\} \)
