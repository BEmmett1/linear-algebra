Show how to find the solution set for the following vector equation

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 4 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] + x_{4} \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ -5 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] .\]

Answer:

\[\mathrm{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 0 & -1 & 3 & 3 & -2 \\ 1 & 0 & 4 & -5 & 2 \\ 1 & 0 & 4 & -4 & 2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & 4 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

The solution set is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -4 \, a + 2 \\ 3 \, a + 2 \\ a \\ 0 \end{array}\right] \middle|\,a\in\mathbb{R}\right\} \)
