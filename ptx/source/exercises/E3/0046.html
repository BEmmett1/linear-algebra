Show how to find the solution set for the following vector equation

\[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -2 \\ 0 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] .\]

Answer:

\[\mathrm{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & -1 & 0 & 0 \\ -2 & -3 & 1 & -2 \\ 0 & -2 & 2 & -4 \\ -5 & -8 & 3 & -6 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 1 & -2 \\ 0 & 1 & -1 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

The solution set is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -a - 2 \\ a + 2 \\ a \end{array}\right] \middle|\,a\in\mathbb{R}\right\} \)
