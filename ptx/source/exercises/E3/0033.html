Show how to find the solution set for the following system of linear equations.

\begin{alignat*}{5} x_{1} &-& 3 \, x_{2} &+& x_{3} &+& 5 \, x_{4} &=& 3 \\-x_{1} &+& 3 \, x_{2} &-& x_{3} &-& 4 \, x_{4} &=& -2 \\ & & &-& x_{3} &-& 3 \, x_{4} &=& -1 \\ \end{alignat*}

Answer:

\[\mathrm{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -3 & 1 & 5 & 3 \\ -1 & 3 & -1 & -4 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & -3 & -1 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -3 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]

The solution set is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \, a \\ a \\ -2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] \middle|\,a\in\mathbb{R}\right\} \)
