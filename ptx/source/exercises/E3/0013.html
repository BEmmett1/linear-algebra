Show how to find the solution set for the following system of linear equations.

\begin{alignat*}{5} x_{1} &-& 3 \, x_{2} &-& 3 \, x_{3} &-& 4 \, x_{4} &=& -5 \\ & & & & x_{3} &+& 2 \, x_{4} &=& 2 \\x_{1} &-& 3 \, x_{2} &-& x_{3} & & &=& -1 \\ \end{alignat*}

Answer:

\[\mathrm{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -3 & -3 & -4 & -5 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 2 & 2 \\ 1 & -3 & -1 & 0 & -1 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -3 & 0 & 2 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 2 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

The solution set is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \, a - 2 \, b + 1 \\ a \\ -2 \, b + 2 \\ b \end{array}\right] \middle|\,a\text{\texttt{,}}b\in\mathbb{R}\right\} \)
