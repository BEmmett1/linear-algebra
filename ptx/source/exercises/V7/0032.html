Consider the subspace

\[W=\operatorname{span} \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -4 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -4 \\ -1 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] \right\} .\]

  1. Explain how to find a basis of \(W\).
  2. Explain how to find the dimension of \(W\).

Answer:

\[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccccc} 0 & -2 & 1 & 2 & -4 & 2 \\ -1 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 & -4 \\ -4 & 3 & -4 & -2 & 1 & -1 \\ -4 & 2 & 2 & 1 & 0 & -2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccccc} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 4 & -\frac{23}{5} \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 7 & -\frac{43}{5} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 & \frac{44}{15} \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & 6 & -\frac{136}{15} \end{array}\right] \]

  1. A basis of \(W\) is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] \right\} \).
  2. The dimension of \(W\) is \( 4 \).
