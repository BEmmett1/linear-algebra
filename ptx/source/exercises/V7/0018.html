Consider the subspace

\[W=\operatorname{span} \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 18 \\ -13 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] \right\} .\]

  1. Explain how to find a basis of \(W\).
  2. Explain how to find the dimension of \(W\).

Answer:

\[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{ccccc} 0 & -2 & 2 & 0 & -2 \\ -3 & -3 & 2 & -3 & 18 \\ 3 & -4 & 2 & 2 & -13 \\ 3 & -1 & -4 & 0 & 5 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{ccccc} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & -3 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & -3 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. A basis of \(W\) is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] \right\} \).
  2. The dimension of \(W\) is \( 4 \).
