Consider the subspace

\[W=\operatorname{span} \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -8 \\ 0 \\ 5 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] \right\} .\]

  1. Explain how to find a basis of \(W\).
  2. Explain how to find the dimension of \(W\).

Answer:

\[\operatorname{RREF} \left[\begin{array}{cccc} -1 & -2 & 6 & -8 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & 0 & 5 \\ -1 & 2 & -2 & -4 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 0 & -2 & 6 \\ 0 & 1 & -2 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. A basis of \(W\) is \( \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] \right\} \).
  2. The dimension of \(W\) is \( 2 \).
