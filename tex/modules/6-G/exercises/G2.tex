\begin{problem}{G2}
Compute the determinant of the matrix $\begin{bmatrix} 3 & -1 & 0 & 4 \\ 2 & 1 & 1& -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & 3 \\ 1 & -2 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
\end{problem}
\begin{solution}
$$\det \begin{bmatrix} 3 & -1 & 0 & 4 \\ 2 & 1 & 1 &-1 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & 3 \\ 1 & -2 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} = -36$$
\end{solution}
